# Comparative Ratio & DuPont Analysis: United Spirits vs Radico Khaitan (FY2021–2024)

This project compares the financial performance of United Spirits Ltd (USL) and Radico Khaitan Ltd using key ratios and DuPont analysis across profitability, efficiency, solvency, and liquidity metrics.

The analysis highlights trends and relative strengths, such as USL's improving net profit margins (12.3% in FY2024) vs Radico's (7.2%), and DuPont breakdowns showing ROE drivers.

## Summary Findings 
- **Profitability**: USL shows stronger net profit margins (12.3% FY2024) and EBITDA margins (16.0%) compared to Radico (7.2% and 11.2%).
- **Efficiency**: USL has higher asset turnover (1.12 FY2024) but lower inventory turnover than Radico.
- **Solvency**: Both have low debt-equity ratios, but USL's is near 0, indicating minimal leverage.
- **Liquidity**: USL's current ratio (1.93 FY2024) is solid; Radico's is 1.63.
- **DuPont ROE**: USL's ROE (20.9% FY2024) driven by high margins; Radico's (13.5%) by moderate leverage and turnover.
- Trends: Both improved post-COVID, with USL outperforming in profitability.
